AGENDA:
Review and approval of the Design Rule Checking (DRC) and Layout Versus Schematic (LVS) reports for the SCL-555 High-Speed Automotive Controller baseline layout prior to mask writing and silicon handoff for Lot 1.

DISCUSSION TOPICS & ANALYSIS:

1. Photolithography Process Window
Dr. Sharma presented the Focus Exposure Matrix (FEM) simulation data for the 0.25 um Metal 1 spacing layout. The simulation was conducted utilizing the i-line 365nm stepper with a numerical aperture (NA) of 0.60. The results demonstrate a comfortable process window with a depth of focus (DOF) of 0.15 um at a nominal exposure dose of 220 mJ/cm2. The photoresist profile has an average sidewall angle of 88.5 degrees, which is sufficient for anisotropic plasma etching.

2. Dry-Etch Chamber Uniformity and Chemistry
Mr. Verma reported on the baseline dry-etch process parameters. He noted that the Dry-Etch-04 chamber (Applied Materials Centura platform) was recently calibrated. A series of test runs utilizing dummy oxide wafers coated with Al-Cu films showed cross-wafer etch rate uniformity within 3.2%. The dry-etch recipe CL_ETCH_M1_0.25 will be utilized as the baseline process for the Metal 1 layer. This recipe employs a chlorine-based chemistry with a flow rate ratio of 80 sccm Cl2 and 40 sccm BCl3, coupled with an RF source power of 800W and a bias power of 150W to ensure clean trench profiles.

3. Reliability and Quality Checkpoints
Ms. Iyer reviewed the quality checklist. Because the SCL-555 is slated for automotive safety certifications, she requested that Lot 1 undergo full reliability screening, including 1000 hours of High-Temperature Operational Life (HTOL) and thermal stress cycling. She confirmed that the dicing and wire bonding parameters are compliant with packaging standards.

DECISIONS & DISPOSITION:
1. The SCL-555 baseline layout design database is approved for handoff.
2. Lot 1 (Lot ID: LOT-2026-01) is cleared for mask writing and immediate fabrication start.
3. The dry-etch process for Metal 1 is locked to recipe CL_ETCH_M1_0.25 on the Dry-Etch-04 chamber.
