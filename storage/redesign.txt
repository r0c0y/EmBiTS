POST-EXCURSION REDESIGN REVIEW: LOT 2 (LOT-2026-02)

Attendees: Dr. A. K. Sharma, Mr. R. K. Verma, Ms. S. Iyer

AGENDA:
Root cause review of Lot 2 yield excursion and layout revision planning.

DISCUSSION:
1. Yield excursion confirmed at 45.8% due to M1 bridging shorts
2. RIE lag identified as primary mechanism on Dry-Etch-04 with legacy CL_ETCH_M1_0.25 recipe
3. TCAD simulations show that for 0.18um spacing with 5500A M1 thickness, the aspect ratio of 3.05:1 requires 25% longer etch time

DECISIONS:
1. Metal 1 width revised back to 0.22um for Lot 3, spacing to 0.25um
2. New etch recipe CL_ETCH_M1_0.18 qualified for future 0.18um-node designs
3. Process Change Notice (PCN-2026-001) issued for dry-etch toolset
