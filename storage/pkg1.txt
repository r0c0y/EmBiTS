BACK-END ASSEMBLY PACKAGING & RELIABILITY INSPECTION REPORT: LOT 1
This report details the back-end assembly operations and mechanical integrity inspections completed for SCL-555 Lot 1 wafers.

1. PACKAGING FLOW DETAILS
- Wafer Batch: SCL-555-MCU-V1, Lot 1 (25 wafers)
- Package Type: 64-pin LQFP (Low-profile Quad Flat Package, copper leadframe).
- Die Attach Epoxy: Ablebond 84-1LMISR4 conductive silver epoxy.
- Wire Type: 1.0 mil (25 um) diameter Gold (Au) wire.
- Molding Compound: Sumitomo G700 Epoxy Molding Compound (EMC).

2. ASSEMBLY PROCESS MEASUREMENTS
- Wafer Dicing: Diced utilizing dicing saw Disco DFD6340 with diamond blades. Dicing yield was 99.85%.
- Die Attach Inspection:
  - Curing parameters: Cured at 150C for 60 minutes in nitrogen oven.
  - Void Analysis (Scanning Acoustic Microscopy - CSAM): Average void area measured at 4.2% of die area. Specification is < 10%.
- Wire Bonding: Completed on K&S IConn high-speed wire bonder. Gold wire ball bonding parameters:
  - Average wire pull strength: 8.4 grams (Requirement: > 3.0g).
  - Average ball shear force: 12.5 grams (Requirement: > 8.0g).
  - Bond pull failure mode: 100% mid-span wire breaks, zero interface lifts.
  - Test sample size: 100 units from 5 different wafers.

3. MOLDING & SEALING
- Transfer Molding: Performed at 175C mold temperature, 90 seconds clamp time. Post-mold cure (PMC) executed at 175C for 4 hours.
- Visual Inspection: 0% voiding or lead frame deformation observed.
- Package Hermeticity (Fine/Gross Leak Testing): Passed standard MIL-STD-883 leak testing.

DISPOSITION:
Lot 1 packaging assembly has met all mechanical and interface specifications. The wire-bond shear force averages 12.5g and wire pull strength averages 8.4g, showing strong joint stability. Wafers are approved to proceed to Quality Qualification.
