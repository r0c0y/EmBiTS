DESIGN RULE MANUAL AND DEVICE SPECIFICATION REPORT: SCL-555
This specifications document outlines the layout parameters and baseline design rules for the SCL-555 High-Speed Automotive Controller. The fabrication of this device will be executed using the Semiconductor Complex Limited (SCL) standard CMOS technology process.

1. TECHNOLOGY PROFILE
- Node Reference: SCL-CMOS-0.25G (Double-metal, single-poly baseline).
- Substrate Type: P-type (100) silicon, 10-15 Ohm-cm resistivity.
- Well Configuration: Twin-tub process with Retrograde N-well.
- Core Voltage: 2.5V nominal supply voltage.

2. METAL 1 INTERCONNECT RULESET
- Minimum Metal 1 Width (M1_W_min): 0.25 um (micro-meters).
- Minimum Metal 1 Spacing (M1_S_min): 0.25 um.
- Metal 1 Thickness: 5500 Angstroms (Al-Cu alloy).
- Sheet Resistance Target (Rs_M1): 0.08 +/- 0.01 Ohm/sq.

3. METAL 2 INTERCONNECT RULESET
- Minimum Metal 2 Width (M2_W_min): 0.35 um.
- Minimum Metal 2 Spacing (M2_S_min): 0.35 um.
- Metal 2 Thickness: 9000 Angstroms.
- Sheet Resistance Target (Rs_M2): 0.05 +/- 0.01 Ohm/sq.

4. PARAMETRIC YIELD TARGETS
- Target Wafer Sort Yield (WSY): Minimum 90.0% acceptable baseline.
- Parametric CD Deviation: Max +/- 0.02 um across all critical lithography levels.
- Gate Oxide Thickness (Tox): 55 Angstroms +/- 3 Angstroms.

CRITICAL DISPOSITION RULE:
Any deviations from the specified Metal 1 line width or spacing configurations must be formally approved by the TCAD modeling department (supervised by Dr. A. K. Sharma). Such changes require comprehensive photolithography and etch process simulation using Synopsys TCAD before mask tooling tape-out.
